You spend 10 minutes before a call trying to remember what you promised that client in your last meeting. You search through email, review WhatsApp messages, open the project document...

You end up joining the call with half the information and a feeling of not being in control.

This isn't normal. And it doesn't have to be this way.

## Why we lose context

Freelancers manage many simultaneous relationships, each at a different stage. And the information about each client is scattered across multiple places: the email where you agreed on the price, the message where they sent materials, the Drive folder with deliverables, the mental note that "this client prefers short Friday calls."

When that information is distributed across four different tools, recovering it before each interaction consumes time and energy. And if something gets lost in retrieval, the quality of the conversation suffers.

## The solution: an interaction log

The piece that most transforms client management isn't the CRM itself. It's the interaction log.

Every time you speak with a client — a call, a meeting, an important email — spend two minutes noting in Notion:

- Date and type of contact
- What was discussed or decided
- Agreed next steps
- Important notes (conversation tone, client concerns, etc.)

That's it. Two minutes after each interaction.

The next time you're about to speak with that client, open their Notion record and in 30 seconds you have the complete context of the entire relationship.

## What changes in practice

When you have this log, you stop arriving at calls with partial information. You can reference previous conversations precisely ("in our call on the 15th you mentioned the deadline was flexible"). The client perceives that you pay attention and have their project under control.

That perception is worth money. Clients who feel well-served come back and refer others.

## How to set it up in Notion

The Interactions database has these basic fields:

- **Client** (relation to the Clients database)
- **Date**
- **Type** (call / email / meeting / message)
- **Summary** (free text with what was discussed)
- **Next steps** (checkboxes or text)

From each client's record, you have a filtered view showing only their interactions, sorted by date. The complete history of the relationship, in one place.
